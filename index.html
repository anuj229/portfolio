About Us


Vintage Motorcar Company Ltd first opened its doors in 1985 with a dream of restoring and trading in the finest and rarest of classic and performance cars. Our Company soon began producing national show winning restorations and record sales. our facility had grown and sales began to include all makes and models of muscle cars, always staying true to our goal of producing the best.
